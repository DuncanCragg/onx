### Discover the Digital Living Space

A "Data-First" and "Local-First" project, part of the "Tools for Thought" or "Personal
Knowledge Management" wave; partly inspired by Ink & Switch and their friends.

See the [Digital Living Space](https://ambient-digital.space) website for my vision.

------

ONX includes both:

 - a graph database of data objects, including state handling and update propagation
 - a tablet-sized touchscreen for exploring and updating those data objects

------

The first demo I'm working on is a family digital notepad built on the ESP32-P4 Seeed
Studio reTerminal D1001 and the M5Stack Tab5 tablets.

Here, the data objects are local hypertext such as notes, messages and documents along with
lists and collections of those, all interlinked and shared over the family WiFi.

This notepad can be your first view into the Digital Living Space.
