### Discover your Digital Living Space

A "Data-First" and "Local-First" project, part of the "Tools for Thought" or "Personal
Knowledge Management" wave; inspired by Ink & Switch and their friends.

See the [Digital Living Space](https://ambient-digital.space) website for the vision.

------

ONX includes both:

 - a graph database of data objects, including state handling and update propagation
 - a tablet-sized touchscreen for exploring and updating those data objects

------

The first demo I'm working on is a digital notepad built on the ESP32-P4 Seeed Studio
reTerminal D1001 and the M5Stack Tab5 tablets.

This digital notepad is your first view in to the future Digital Living Space.

In this notepad, the data objects are local hypertext such as notes, documents and lists
and collections of those, all interlinked.
